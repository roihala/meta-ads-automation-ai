## Data Deletion — How to Request

**Campaigner** is an internal tool Aiweon uses to manage its own Meta advertising activity. The data Campaigner holds consists of:

- Aggregated performance data for Aiweon's own Meta ad campaigns.
- Business Manager context (ad accounts, Pages, Pixels) owned by Aiweon.
- Page engagement aggregates on posts promoted by Aiweon.
- Audit logs of automated proposals and human operator approvals.

Campaigner does **not** collect personal data about individuals who view or interact with Aiweon's ads.

### How to request deletion

Send an email to **admin@aiweon.co.il** with the subject line **"Data Deletion Request"** and include:

1. Your name and contact email.
2. A description of the data you want deleted (for example, the specific Meta ad account, Page, or time range).
3. Evidence of your relationship to the data — for example, confirmation that you are the owner of the Meta Business Manager, ad account, or Page that sourced the data.

### What we will do

1. We will acknowledge your request within **5 business days**.
2. We will verify the requester's authority to request deletion.
3. We will delete the identified data from Campaigner's database within **30 days** of verification.
4. We will confirm completion by email.

### Retention exceptions

The audit log of automated proposals and operator approvals is retained indefinitely for internal traceability and compliance review, even when source data is deleted. Audit entries are stripped of any personal identifiers; they record only: decision type, timestamp, operator identity, and reference to ad-account-level objects.

### Scope

This process applies to data held by Campaigner. It does **not** control data held by Meta Platforms, which remains subject to [Meta's own data-deletion processes](https://www.facebook.com/help/delete_account).

### Questions

For any questions about this process, contact **admin@aiweon.co.il**.
